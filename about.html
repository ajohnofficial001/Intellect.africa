Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students in Africa by providing access to global opportunities, mentorship, and skill-building programs

We envision a future where African students have the knowledge, resources, and support needed to maximize their potential and thrive in their educational journeys.

Why Intellect Africa?

Intellect Africa has evolved significantly since its inception in 2022. What started as a mission to help high school students gain access to and learn about incredible opportunities has now grown into a structured mentorship and scholarship initiative.

The first major milestone was launching a college application mentorship program, guiding students applying to schools in the United States. This later evolved into a mentorship program for high school seniors, incorporating scholarship support. Over a six-month period, the program operated in three active and three passive phases, pairing students with mentors—current college students—who provided guidance throughout the process. As a result, the top three performers were awarded scholarships towards their education.

Now, Intellect Africa is expanding its mission beyond just application support. We are dedicated to helping students studying in Nigeria maximize their full potential by fostering academic excellence, skill-building, and innovation. Our focus is on equipping young Africans with the resources and guidance necessary to thrive in their educational journeys and contribute meaningfully to the continent’s growth.
